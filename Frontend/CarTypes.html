Don't dream it, drive it!

Going with friend or family in a trip? Or you want to enjoy a luxury trip by yourself?

Pick the suitable car type!

Economy

Good for city driving and suitable for four people for shorter trips, and two or three people for longer trips.

Number of people: 5 (3 adults + 2 children)

Luggage: 1 large suitcase + 1 small suitcase

Mini

Suitable for three or four people for very short trips. For longer trips, they’re more suitable for one or two people. Minis are often (but not necessarily) hatchbacks.

Number of people: 4 (2 adults + 2 children)

Luggage: 2 small suitcases

Compact

Medium-sized, often (but not always) hatchback, and are good for four people for a shorter trip - and two or three people for a longer trip.

Number of people: 5 (3 adults + 2 children)

Luggage: 1 large suitcase + 1 small suitcase


Intermediate

Bigger than ‘compact’ cars, and smaller than ‘standard’ cars.

Number of people: 5 (4 adults + 1 children)

Luggage: 2 large suitcase + 1 small suitcase

Convertible

Cars with a soft roof that you can put up if the weather isn’t great or down if you want to enjoy the breeze and the sun.

Number of people: 2-4 adults depending on size

Luggage: 1-2 large suitcases

SUV

Good-sized vehicles with a rugged look, set higher off the road than normal cars.

Number of people: 5 adults

Luggage: 2 large suitcase


Standard

Larger sedan, provides more room for passengers and can fit an additional bag in the trunk compared to an intermediate car.

Number of people: 5 adults

Luggage: 2 large suitcase + 1 small suitcase

Full-size

More passenger room and luggage space than a smaller car offers.

Number of people: 5 adults

Luggage: 2 large suitcase + 2 small suitcase

Luxury

Great for travelling in style. These are cars made by high-end brands with luxury features: a sleek look, comfy seats, plush interior and powerful engine.

Number of people: 5 adults

Luggage: 2 large suitcase + 3 small suitcase


Luxury

Large cars, often with more headroom and luggage space than most cars.

Number of people: 9 or 7, depending on car

Luggage: 9 or 7, depending on car
